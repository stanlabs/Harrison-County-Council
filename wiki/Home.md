# County Council for Harrison County, Chesapeake

`LEGISinfo` contain the original text of [ordinances](https://github.com/stanlabs/Harrison-County-Council/tree/main/LEGISinfo/Ordinances), [motions and resolutions](https://github.com/stanlabs/Harrison-County-Council/tree/main/LEGISinfo/Resolutions%20and%20Motions), and [tabled documents](https://github.com/stanlabs/Harrison-County-Council/tree/main/LEGISinfo/Tabled%20Documents) passed by Council.

`Procedural Info` contains information on the practice and procedure of Council, including [rules of procedure](https://github.com/stanlabs/Harrison-County-Council/blob/main/Procedural%20Info/Rules%20of%20Proceeding.md) and [decisions of the chair](https://github.com/stanlabs/Harrison-County-Council/blob/main/Procedural%20Info/Selected%20Decisions%20of%20the%20Chair.md).

The [wiki](https://github.com/stanlabs/Harrison-County-Council/wiki) is an index of ordinances and their amendments. For some important ordinances, there will be a consolidation which shows the current, in force text, with all amendments made. Please note that if a consolidation and the original ordinance conflicts, the original ordinance has the force of law.
