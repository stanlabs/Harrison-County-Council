# CR. 2-3 | Resolution to Authorize Councilor Glarpenn's Absence

*Introduced July 1st, 2026*

---

IN THE COUNCIL OF HARRISON COUNTY

Mr. glarpenn introduced the following Resolution to the Council
# A RESOLUTION
To authorize the temporary absence of Councilor Glarpenn pursuant to Section 27 of the County Charter

*Be it enacted by the County Council in accordance with Section 24 of the County Charter,*

*WHEREAS, Section 27 of the County Charter permits the Council to authorize the prolonged
absence of a Councilor; and*

*WHEREAS, Councilor Glarpenn will be absent from Council sittings for a period of two weeks
beginning July 2, 2026, while remaining available to communicate with the Council and
continue legislative work;*

*NOW THEREFORE, by virtue of the power and authority vested in the Harrison County
Council in accordance with Section 24 of the County Charter, the Council hereby resolves that
Councilor Glarpenn's absence from Council sittings for the period beginning July 2, 2026, and
ending July 16, 2026, is hereby authorized and shall not constitute grounds for expulsion under
Section 27 of the County Charter.*
