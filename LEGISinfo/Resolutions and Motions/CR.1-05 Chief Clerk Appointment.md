# CR 1-5 | Chief Clerk Appointment

*Introduced June 20, 2026*

---

IN THE COUNCIL OF HARRISON COUNTY

Mr. Vinnyvxx introduced the following Resolution to the Council
# A RESOLUTION
Appoint Vinnyvxx as Chief Clerk of the Harrison County Council; granting access to necessary records, documents, systems, and platforms; and authorizing the management of Council clerical personnel.

*WHEREAS, the timely processing, docketing, maintenance, and administration of legislative
business is essential to the effective operation of the Harrison County Council; and*

*WHEREAS, recent delays and deficiencies in the performance of clerical functions have
hindered the efficient conduct of Council business; and*

*WHEREAS, Councilman Vinnyvxx is an active member of the Council and possesses
substantial legislative and clerical experience, including service as a legislative clerk in other
governmental communities and over one year of service as a Senate Clerk in Clark County; and*

*WHEREAS, the Council finds it to be in the best interests of Harrison County to appoint a
qualified individual to oversee and administer the Council's clerical operations;*

*NOW THEREFORE, by the virtue of the power and authority vested in the Harrison County
the County Council in accordance with Section 24 of the County Charter, do hereby resolve the
following effective immediately:*

---

(a) There is hereby established the position of Chief Clerk of the Harrison
County Council. The Chief Clerk shall serve as the principal
administrative and clerical officer of the County Council and shall be
responsible for the management, supervision, and administration of all
legislative clerical functions, including the preparation, receipt,
maintenance, publication, organization, and docketing of legislation,
resolutions, records, reports, proceedings, and other official Council
documents, and shall exercise all powers and duties provided by this Resolution and any applicable law, rule, policy, or directive of the County
Council.

(b) Councilman Vinnyvxx is hereby appointed to the position of Chief Clerk
of the Harrison County Council, effective immediately.

- (i) Notwithstanding any other provision of County law, ordinance,
rule, policy, or practice, the position of Chief Clerk shall not be
deemed incompatible with membership on the County Council,
and Councilman Vinnyvxx may simultaneously hold and exercise
the powers and duties of both offices.

(c) The Chief Clerk shall be granted full and unrestricted access to any and all
records, documents, files, archives, databases, communications, software,
systems, platforms, servers, channels, and other resources reasonably
necessary to perform the duties of the office. All County officers,
employees, departments, boards, commissions, and agents shall cooperate
with and provide such access upon request.

(d) The Chief Clerk shall have authority over all clerical personnel assigned to
or serving the County Council and may recruit, appoint, supervise, train,
assign duties to, discipline, suspend, remove, or otherwise manage such
personnel as the Chief Clerk deems necessary for the efficient
administration of Council operations.

(e) The County Executive, all County departments, and all County personnel
are directed to take any actions necessary to implement and effectuate this
Resolution.
