# CR. 2-4 | Resolution to Appoint Stanlabs PPT

*Introduced July 1st, 2026*

---

IN THE COUNCIL OF HARRISON COUNTY

Mr. A0IM introduced the following Resolution to the Council
# A RESOLUTION
To appoint Stanlabs to President pro tempore of the County Council.

*Be it enacted by the County Council in accordance with Section 24 of the County Charter,*

*WHEREAS, Section 27 of the County Charter mandates the election of one the Councilor to be
the President pro tempore of the Council as its first order of business of a new term;*

*NOW THEREFORE, by the virtue of the power and authority vested in the Harrison County
the County Council in accordance with Section 24 of the County Charter, do hereby resolve the
following effective immediately*

---

(a) That Stanlabs shall be appointed to the position of President Pro Tempore.
